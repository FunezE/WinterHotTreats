Grinch's Matcha

Key Ingredients

  • 💚 Matcha powder (1 tsp)
  • 🔵 Milk (1 cup)
  • 🍯 Honey or sugar (1 tsp, to taste)
  • 🟤 Vanilla extract (1/4 tsp)

Equipment

  • Whisk or matcha whisk
  • Saucepan

Quick Guide

  1. In a small bowl, whisk matcha powder with 2 tbsp of warm water until smooth and lump-free.
  2. Heat milk gently in a saucepan over medium heat (don’t boil).
  3. Stir in honey (or sugar) and vanilla extract.
  4. Whisk the matcha mixture into the milk until fully combined and frothy.
  5. Serve in a mug and enjoy the Grinch’s green magic! 💚☕
