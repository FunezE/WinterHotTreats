Chai Delight

Key Ingredients

  • 🟤 Black tea (1 bag or 1 tsp loose-leaf)
  • 🎄 Milk (1 cup)
  • 🟤 Ground cinnamon (1/2 tsp)
  • 🍥 Ground ginger (1/4 tsp)
  • 🟤 Ground cardamom (1/8 tsp)
  • 🍯 Honey or sugar (to taste)

Equipment

  • Saucepan
  • Strainer

Quick Guide

  1. Bring 1/2 cup water to a simmer in a saucepan. Add black tea and spices (cinnamon, ginger, cardamom).
  2. Simmer for 3–4 minutes to infuse flavors.
  3. Add milk and heat gently, stirring occasionally.
  4. Strain into a mug, sweeten with honey or sugar, and stir.
  5. Enjoy your aromatic chai moment! 🍵✨
