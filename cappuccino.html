Quick Guide

  1. Brew a shot of espresso and pour it into a mug.
  2. Steam the milk until it's frothy and hot (but not boiling).
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, leaving room for a thick layer of milk foam.
  4. Top with a generous amount of milk foam.
  5. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon and a touch of nutmeg for a holiday spice twist.
