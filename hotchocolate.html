Winter Mint Hot Chocolate

Key Ingredients

  • 🍫 Cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
  • 🍬 Sugar (2 tbsp)
  • 🥛 Milk (2 cups)
  • 🧂 Salt (pinch)
  • 🌿 Peppermint extract (1/4 tsp)
  • 🍥 Ground cinnamon (1/2 tsp)
  • 🍦 Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional for topping)

Equipment

  • Saucepan
  • Whisk

Quick Guide

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together cocoa powder, sugar, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of milk to create a smooth paste.
  3. Gradually whisk in the remaining milk and heat gently over medium heat (don’t boil).
  4. Stir in peppermint extract for a festive flavor.
  5. Serve in a mug and optionally top with whipped cream or marshmallows.
  6. Enjoy your comforting hot chocolate!
