London Fog Latte

Key Ingredients

  • 🟤 Earl Grey tea (1 bag or 1 tsp loose-leaf)
  • 🎄 Steamed milk (1 cup)
  • 🍯 Honey or sugar (1 tsp, to taste)
  • 🟤 Vanilla extract (1/4 tsp)

Equipment

  • Teapot or saucepan
  • Steamer or frother

Quick Guide

  1. Brew Earl Grey tea in 1/2 cup of hot water for 3–5 minutes.
  2. Heat and froth milk until hot and creamy.
  3. Stir honey and vanilla extract into the brewed tea.
  4. Pour steamed milk over the tea, leaving room for a little foam on top.
  5. Relish the soothing flavors of a classic London Fog! ☁️🍵
